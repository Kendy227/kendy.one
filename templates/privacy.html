01. Information We Collect

We collect specific telemetry data necessary for transaction processing and account authentication. This includes your Player Identifier (UID), email address for communication, and transaction metadata. We do not store sensitive payment card details directly on our servers; all processing is handled via secure, PCI-compliant gateways.

02. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is utilized strictly for service delivery optimization. This includes verifying top-up successful delivery, preventing fraudulent activities, and providing personalized customer support. Your Player ID is used solely to facilitate the digital credit transfer to the intended gaming environment.

03. Information Sharing

Xtreme Top-up operates on a strict "Zero Sell" policy. We do not trade or sell your personal information to third-party marketing firms. Information is only disclosed to official gaming publishers and payment clearinghouses to the extent required to complete your requested transaction.

04. Data Security

Our infrastructure is fortified with multi-layered security protocols. This includes end-to-end encryption for data in transit and rest, firewall-protected server clusters, and routine vulnerability scanning. We implement industry-leading standards to ensure your digital assets and identity remain uncompromised.

05. Cookies and Tracking

We utilize low-impact session cookies to maintain your login state and enhance navigation speed. These technologies allow us to understand user flow patterns within the Xtreme Top-up platform, enabling us to refine the user interface and detect suspicious login attempts from unauthorized locations.

06. Your Rights

As a Xtreme Top-up user, you maintain full sovereignty over your data. You have the right to request a complete audit of the data we hold, the right to rectify inaccuracies, and the right to request full account erasure (Right to be Forgotten). Contact our Privacy Officer through the support hub to initiate these protocols.
