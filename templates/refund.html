Age Restriction & Consent

This platform is not intended for users under the age of 18. Any utilization of our services by minors is strictly at your own risk. If you are under 18, you must obtain explicit permission from a parent or legal guardian before initiating any transaction. Xtreme Top-up reserves the right to request proof of age or parental consent at any time.

01. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are exclusively authorized for transactions where the digital goods or credits have failed to arrive at the designated account due to systemic failure or technical anomalies on the Xtreme Top-up side. Eligibility is determined through an internal audit of the transaction logs and publisher delivery confirmations.

02. Refund Process

To initiate a claim, users must contact our Support Center within 24 hours of the original transaction timestamp. You must provide a valid Transaction ID, Player ID, and proof of non-delivery (e.g., in-game wallet screenshots). Our Compliance Team investigates all claims within 72 hours.

03. Refund Methods

Approved refunds are typically reverted to the original payment source (Credit Card, Digital Wallet, etc.). In cases where the original payment method is unavailable for reversal, Xtreme Top-up may issue a digital credit to your internal Xtreme Top-up Balance for future use.

04. Non-Refundable Items

Digital assets that have been successfully provisioned to your account and confirmed as "Delivered" by the game publisher are strictly non-refundable. Additionally, Xtreme Top-up does not issue refunds for user-side errors, such as providing an incorrect Player ID or UID during checkout.

05. Processing Time

Once a refund is authorized by our team, the transfer is initiated immediately. However, financial institutions typically take 3 to 10 business days to reflect the funds in your balance. This timeframe is dictated by your bank and is outside the control of the Xtreme Top-up platform.
